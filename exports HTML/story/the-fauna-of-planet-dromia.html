The Fauna of planet Dromia

Planet Dromia is a very biologically diverse planet, that has biomes going from lush oceans and forests to lung rotting mushroom jungles and gigantic calcified tree structures beneath it all.

The animals ->
Just like in the Permian period of earth's development, the Dromia's fauna has massive insect like animals that have evolved into massive sized creatures, that are most of the time friendly or can even be tamed, although some more predator like creatures like to live in swarms making them extremely dangerous to the human player or any unfortunate animal that wanders into their hive or the area surrounding it
the plants ->
some plants in planet Dromia have learned to sustain themselves with something that isnt sunlight and minerals in the soil, they have the ability to slowly move by having muscle like structures that allow them to travel long distances to hunt food or become a parasite for a massive tree for example.

the fungi biomes->
The fungi fauna in Dromia has expanded into their own biomes due to high levels of radiation caused by natural redstone isotopes in the minerals surrounding them. These fungi jungles with massive tree like shroom structures and lush vegetation from an outside perspective can look like a paradise, but in reality without proper equipment your lungs would rot within minutes due to the extreme amounts of spores and other air based microorganisms. (taken a lot of inspiration from Nausica, a Ghibli studio movie)
